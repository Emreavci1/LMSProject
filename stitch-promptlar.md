# Stitch Promptları — LÖSEV LMS

> Kullanım: Her ekran için "ANA STİL" bloğunu + o ekranın bloğunu birlikte kopyalayıp Stitch'e yapıştır.
> Palet: LÖSEV logo renklerinin (mavi #00AEEF, kırmızı #E20A17) modernize edilmiş hali —
> Ana mavi #0284C7, vurgu kırmızısı #DC2626, sidebar #0F172A, zemin #F8FAFC

---

## 🎨 ANA STİL (her prompta başa ekle)

Design a modern corporate LMS (learning management system) web app for LOSEV,
a Turkish children's health foundation. Color palette: deep sky blue (#0284C7)
as the primary color for buttons, links and active states; modern red
(#DC2626) used very sparingly for key accents and alerts only. Dark
navy-slate (#0F172A) for the sidebar, heading text #1E293B, body text
#475569, soft off-white background (#F8FAFC), white cards with subtle
shadows, 12px rounded corners and light #E2E8F0 borders. Green (#16A34A)
for success states like free badges and completed checkmarks. Clean,
elegant, easy on the eyes but rich in detail — corporate and trustworthy,
with a warm human touch fitting a children's charity. Turkish UI text.
Left sidebar navigation layout with the user's avatar, name and role at
the top. Modern sans-serif typography, generous whitespace, subtle dividers.

---

## 1 — Öğrenci: Eğitimleri Keşfet

Student panel: "Egitimleri Kesfet" (Discover Courses) page. Left sidebar menu
items: Egitimleri Kesfet, Egitimlerim, Bakiyem, Profil, Ayarlar. Main content:
a search bar with filter chips (category, level, duration), a horizontal row
of category pills (Saglik, Teknoloji, Kisisel Gelisim, Sanat), and a
responsive grid of course cards. Each card: cover image, category tag, course
title, instructor name with small avatar, lesson count, duration, and a
"Katil" button. Free courses show a green "Ucretsiz" badge.

---

## 2 — Öğrenci: Eğitimlerim

Student panel: "Egitimlerim" (My Courses) page, same sidebar. A list/grid of
enrolled course cards, each with a progress bar (e.g. 64% tamamlandi),
"Devam Et" button, last accessed lesson name. Top summary strip with 3 stat
tiles: enrolled courses count, completed courses, total learning hours.

---

## 3 — Öğrenci: Ders İzleme (Udemy tarzı)

Course player page like Udemy: large video player area on the left,
collapsible lesson list panel on the right showing sections and lessons with
checkmarks for completed ones and durations. Below the player: tabs for
Genel Bakis, Notlar, Duyurular. Course progress bar at the top.

---

## 4 — Öğrenci: Bakiyem + Profil + Ayarlar

Student panel: "Bakiyem" (My Balance) page: current balance card, transaction
history table (date, description, amount). "Profil" page: profile photo,
name, email, editable info form. "Ayarlar": notification toggles, password
change form. Same sidebar layout.

---

## 5 — Eğitmen: Eğitimlerim + Eğitim Detayı

Instructor panel. Sidebar items: Egitimlerim, Egitim Ac, Program, Profil,
Ayarlar. "Egitimlerim" page: table/cards of the instructor's courses with
student count, rating, status (Aktif/Pasif), edit buttons. "Egitim Detayi"
page: course header with stats (enrolled students, completion rate), tabs
for Icerik (curriculum builder with draggable lesson list), Ogrenciler
(student table with progress %, clicking opens a student detail drawer),
Ayarlar.

---

## 6 — Eğitmen: Eğitim Açma + Program

Instructor panel: "Egitim Ac" (Create Course) page as a multi-step wizard:
step 1 basic info (title, category, description, cover image upload area),
step 2 curriculum (add sections and lessons), step 3 review & publish.
"Program" page: weekly calendar view showing scheduled lessons/sessions.

---

## 7 — Admin: Genel Bakış (Analitik)

Admin panel. Sidebar items: Genel Bakis, Kullanicilar, Egitimler,
Kategoriler, Analitik, Ayarlar. "Genel Bakis" dashboard: KPI stat cards
(toplam kullanici, aktif egitim, tamamlanan ders, aylik aktif ogrenci), a
line chart of enrollments over time, a bar chart of most popular categories,
a recent activity feed, and a table of top courses.

---

## 8 — Admin: Kullanıcılar + Eğitimler Yönetimi

Admin panel: "Kullanicilar" page: user table with avatar, name, email, role
badge (Ogrenci/Egitmen/Yonetici), status, actions; filter bar by role and
status; "Yeni Kullanici" button opening a modal form. "Egitimler" page: all
courses table with instructor, category, student count, status toggle and
detail view.
